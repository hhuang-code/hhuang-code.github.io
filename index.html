About

👤 I am currently a Post Doctoral Associate at the NYU Embodied AI and Robotics Lab and the Center for AI and Robotics (CAIR) at NYU Abu Dhabi (NYUAD). Prior to this, I completed my Ph.D. at the Tandon School of Engineering, New York University (NY City), under the supervision of Prof. Yi Fang. I earned my B.Eng. degree in Digital Media Technology and my M.S. degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, the latter under the supervision of Prof. Huadong Ma, followed by an M.S. degree in Computer Science from University of Rochester, USA, under the supervision of Prof. Chenliang Xu.

My Research

My research is dedicated to advancing robotics that are robust, adaptable, and equitable through the development of intelligent, AI-driven methods, incorporating innovations in 3D computer vision, signal analysis, multimodal foundation models, imitation learning and reinforcement learning to enhance robotic perception, motion and reasoning. By combining theoretical insights with practical applications, I aim to develop robotic systems that prioritize accuracy, efficiency, and safety, enabling intelligent interaction with complex environments.
